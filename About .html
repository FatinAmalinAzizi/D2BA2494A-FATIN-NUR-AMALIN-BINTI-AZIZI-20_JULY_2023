About

Standing Tall Grew Bigger

Was established in 1993 and registered under the Trustee Organization Act 1952. This orphanage defends and provides protection and helps children who have no dependents, especially the orphans and the poor. After more than 30 years of devotion to the local community, YAATIM has contributed a lot and helped many children in distress and also treated them very well.
