CSR Activity Programme From CG Computer Sdn Bhd

The 2022 Eid celebrations hosted by the Islamic Center were filled with joy and community. Families gathered together on the morning of Eid to pray and embrace after the month of fasting and reflection during Ramadan. Children dressed in new holiday clothes played games and enjoyed sweets as the aromas of traditional foods like biryani, kebabs, and gulab jamun wafted through the air. An atmosphere of peace and gratitude permeated the day's festivities. The highlight was the group recitation of takbeer and the heartfelt prayers as everyone united to mark the end of the holy month. With smiles all around, it was clear the 2022 Eid celebrations would leave happy memories for many years to come.

Aidilfitri Celebration Ceremony 2023 Yayasan Kebajikan Anak-Anak Yatim Kelantan (YAATIM)

Guest of Honor Dato Hjh Mumtaz Binti Md Nawi (DJMK)(JMK) as Chairman of the Welfare, Family Development and Women's Committee. This celebration was held to celebrate the children and students of asnaf who are under the care of YAATIM, as well as to strengthen the relationship between YAATIM and donors and the surrounding community.
